Guide to create visualisations for our sites

3rd version - faster, easier to share and more compatible

Add data visualisations, infographics and multimedia to our site. Recommendations on how to share this materials with other sites.

▸ 6 core principles:

Clarity

Communicate clearly to the average citizen. We aim for simplicity instead of graphics for experts.

Any device

Graphs that render properly in any device: desktops, mobiles and tablets.

Any page and forever

Publish on any page of any of our sites and that it stays published forever.

Share with anyone

To be able to share with any site and that they can translate and adjust the graphics.

Consistent with our design

Be consistent with the design of our sites, using a predefined color palette and official fonts.

Openness and collaboration

Encourage and accept external quality collaborations , be more open and transparent in our campaigns. (open and open source).
